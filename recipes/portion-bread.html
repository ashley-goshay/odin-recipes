Portion Bread

Description

No one really knows where portion bread came from. But it's great for when you want something bland, unappetizing, or confusing.

Ingredients

  • Packet of portion bread powder
  • 1 cup of water
  • Mixing bowl

Steps

  1. Open the portion bread powder packet, empty into the mixing bowl
  2. Pour in 1 cup of water to mixing bowl
  3. Mix powder + water mixture for 1 minute until bread materializes.
