Roasted Porg

Description

This aromatic and hearty roasted porg is great for cold nights and winding down from battling the Empire.

Ingredients

  • One Dead Porg
  • Firewood
  • Spices
  • Salt and Pepper

Steps

  1. Build a fire
  2. Add aromatics. Now add the salt and pepper. Rub all over porg meat.
  3. Put porg on roasting stick.
  4. Roast porg over fire until browned.
