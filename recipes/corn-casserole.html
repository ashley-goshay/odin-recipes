Corn Casserole

Description

This no-fail 5 ingredient jiffy corn casserole recipe is versatile and bakes up into a savory side dish that will complement any meal.

Ingredients

  • Jiffy Cornbread Mix
  • 1/2 cup of melted butter
  • 1 can of sweetened corn
  • 1 can of whole kernel, normal corn
  • 1/2 cup of sourcream

Steps

There are only 2 steps to this recipe!

  1. Throw all of those ingredients in a bowl and mix them up.


  2. Transfer the mixture to a greased 8x8 baking pan and bake at 350 degrees for about 45 minutes. The exact baking time will depend on the exact size pan you use, so just be sure to watch the casserole closely. You'll know it is done when the center is completely set.
